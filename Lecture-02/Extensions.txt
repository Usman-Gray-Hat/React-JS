================================
MOST COMMON & USEFUL EXTENSIONS:
================================

1) Babel Javascript (Provides intellisence and make keywords colorful).

2) Javascript es6 code snippets (Provides shortcuts of snippet codes structure).

3) VScode Icons (Provides icon to every file).

4) Bracket Highlighter (Highlights the body of functions and statements).

5) Auto Rename Tag (Helps to rename tag automatically).
